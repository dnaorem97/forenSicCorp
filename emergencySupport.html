What information should we prepare before calling your emergency line?
It's helpful to have basic information about the incident ready, including when it was discovered, which systems are affected, any observed unusual behavior, and whether any containment actions have already been taken. However, don't delay calling if you don't have all details - we can help gather information during the initial assessment.
Is our emergency communication confidential?
Absolutely. All communications regarding your incident are kept strictly confidential. We can work under attorney-client privilege when appropriate and can sign NDAs before beginning work if required.
How do you handle payment for emergency services?
For emergency services, we typically require a retainer before beginning work, with the exception of existing clients with service agreements. We accept major credit cards, wire transfers, and can work with your procurement department for expedited processing during emergencies.
